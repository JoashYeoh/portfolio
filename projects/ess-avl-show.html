AVL Project & Showcase - Lighting Show

Lighting show on the theme of “Born Of Fire”, featuring the song “Blood and Stone” by Audiomachine.

Overview

In this project, my teammate and I were tasked to programme a video and lighting showcase, utilising Pixera as the media server for video projection and GrandMA3 for lighting programming. The whole show was timecoded and synced between the GrandMA3 lighting console and Pixera media server. Hence allowing the show to be synced.

Process

My teammate and I started with brainstorming ideas as to what music we wanted to use as the base idea for the theme that we wanted to achieve. In this case we went with "Blood And Stone" by Audiomachine as it is a music that is dynamic and allowed for controled lows and high impact moments through the strong resulting in a more dynamic and emotional flow of the show.
After deciding on the theme and colour scheme, we consolidated some media assets for the Media Server, while begining the lighting programming via the GrandMA3 console.

Outcome

At the end of this project, our work was awarded the best AVL Show in our class, and gained rather positive comments from industry partners who had an exclusive live expereince of the show, during our Diploma's End Semmester Showcase.
